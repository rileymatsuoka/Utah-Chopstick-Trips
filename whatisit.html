What is a "Chopstick Trip?"

Created in November 2023, a "Chopstick Trip" happens when a group of friends is hungry for spontaneous adventure. They pack their things for 24 hours, not knowing beforehand where they would be going. Then then throw a chopstick (yes, a literal chopstick) at a map of Utah (it can technically be any state). Wherever the chopstick lands becomes their destination to which they must leave immediately. For the next 24 hours, they must find unique places to stay, food to eat, and activities to enjoy that are found in that location.

Wanna try it?

Here are some recommended steps for taking your own Chopstick Trip!

  1. Select Your Destination

    • The random selection method is what makes Chopstick Trip exciting! Get a map of Utah (or any state/area), print it out, and throw a chopstick/dart at it! Try to commit to the idea that you can have fun ANYWHERE!
    • If you don't have a physical map or chopstick, you can spin our online wheel! (See below)

  2. Find Where You Will Stay

    • As soon as you know where you're going, find out where you'll stay! Here are some resources to find quick overnight locations:
        • Airbnb
        Hotels Tonight
    • You can also stay with friends or family members in the area, or camp outside or in your car!

  3. Find Activities

    • Check the city's webpage for local events!
    • Check google maps for attractions and restaurants in the area!
    • Check Facebook groups and ask people where to visit!

Don't be afraid to talk to the people you meet! Not only will they have good recommendations, but you will also make new friends!
